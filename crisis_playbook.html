Crisis Communication Playbook

Turn uncertainty into control

A compact, actionable playbook I use to lead clear, confident responses when it matters most. Built for speed, consistency and reputational protection — suitable for startups, NGOs and corporate teams.

  1. Alert & acknowledge: Confirm incident with facts; notify Crisis Lead and Legal.
  2. Assemble core team: Crisis Lead, Comms Lead, Legal, Ops, HR (if needed).
  3. Triage impact: Safety risk, regulatory exposure, reputation, operational disruption.
  4. Immediate output: Holding statement and internal summary for leadership.

Goal: reduce confusion. Provide consistent, factual updates every 30–60 minutes until stable.

We are aware of an incident affecting [what/where]. Our immediate priorities are the safety of affected people and an accurate understanding of the situation. We are currently assessing the impact and working with relevant authorities. We will provide updated information as soon as possible. For media inquiries: [press@yourorg.com]
